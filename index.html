Where Coffee Becomes an Art

We source single-origin beans from ethical farms across India and beyond, roast them fresh in-house, and craft each cup with precision and passion.

The Roastery

From Bean to Cup

Our roastery is the heart of everything we do. Using a state-of-the-art Probat roaster, we carefully develop each coffee's unique flavor profile.

We roast small batches multiple times per week to ensure maximum freshness. Every bag of beans you take home was roasted within the last 7 days.
